# 📚 Resources

> Curated, specific learning resources — organized to avoid detours.

!!! tip "Philosophy"
    Every resource here is **specific** (not "learn X in 30 days" vague). Free resources are prioritized. Paid resources (Udemy) are listed when they're clearly the best option for the topic — they go on sale for $10–15 regularly.

---

| Platform | Best For | Notes |

| [CS50 (Harvard)](https://cs50.harvard.edu/) | CS fundamentals, Python, Web | Exceptional quality, free forever |
